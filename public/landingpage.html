Our platform detects cheating through advanced AI technology that monitors exam-takers in real-time.

Face Detection

Detects and counts the number of faces in frame to ensure only authorized test-takers are present during the exam.

Gaze Tracking

Monitors eye movement to detect when students look away from the screen, which may indicate use of unauthorized resources.

Face Position Direction

Analyzes the direction the test-taker's face is pointing to identify suspicious head movements during the exam.
